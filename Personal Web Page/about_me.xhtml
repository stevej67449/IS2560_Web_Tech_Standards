My Story

Although I have lived in many different states, I consider my home to be Kansas. I began my college studies there before joining the U.S. Air Force in 2010. For almost my entire military career I was stationed in Maryland working as an Intelligence Analyst.

When I left the service in 2015, I moved to Pittsburgh and started learning web development as a hobby. Eventually I became serious about expanding my development skills further, so I made the decision to pursue a degree in Information Science at the University of Pittsburgh.

I have a strong interest in topics related to user experience (UX), user interaction (UI), usability, and human-computer interaction (HCI).
