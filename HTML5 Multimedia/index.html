A Guide to HTML5 Multimedia

Introduction

Audio and visual media offers a host of benefits not present in written forms of communication. It is easier to convey nonverbal behaviors, tone, intonation, and provide more context to a message without the constraints of written words. Although the web started out dominated by text as the primary mode of transferring information, increased internet speeds have made web-based video and audio multimedia to new heights of popularity. Adding this kind of multimedia to a website can help you engage with users.

Although the term multimedia covers a host of concepts including sound, animation, and movies, this guide will limit the scope to audio and video elements on webpages.

Because we are limiting our guide to these two areas, we will have the opportunity to have a more complete inspection of these subjects.
